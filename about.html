About RKM Communications

RKM Communications is an Entertainment and Consumer PR agency that manages the PR and media for clients from TV and film celebrities, to beauty products and fashion and lifestyle brands. From our expert departments, we ensure that RKM clients are always present with their audience and consumer, through intelligent, strategic and experienced use of print, online and broadcast media.

Founded in 2002, our boutique London PR agency is made up of a team of proven PR experts who have the ability to succeed and the contacts and the resources to act globally. Our work will not only increase your profile and that of your brand or product, but it will also increase your sales and help that ever important bottom line.

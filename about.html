About RKM Communications

Founded in 2002, RKM Communications is a leading London based Entertainment and Consumer PR agency that works with clients from Entertainment, Fashion, Health and Beauty. From our expert departments, we offer a wide range of services to develop, strengthen and maintain the media presence of our clients, ensuring that consumers and key industry figures stay aware of their offerings through intelligent, strategic and experienced use of print, online and broadcast media.

RKM know the importance of being present in the right media with the right message. Our goal is to continually support our clients growth. Strengthening your position with you current audience whilst continuously striving to introduce you and your product or service to a new but ever changing audience and demographic.
